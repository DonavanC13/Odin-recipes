Fettuccinni Alfredo

Fettucinni Alfredo is a simple yet versatile dish. It's ability to be served with many different types of foods is what makes it such a popular dish among many restaurants across the United States whether you pair it with chicken, shrimp, steak or if you'd rather just have plain Alfredo this dish is not only delicious but a heartwarming treat for many!.

In this article we'll be sharing one of my favorite recipes for Fettuccinni Alfredo! This recipe is simply a modified take on the classic Fettuccinni Alfredo everyone knows and loves. With this recipe not only can you make a delicious meal for yourself and your family but it's simple and easy too!

Ingredients

  • Pasta: For this dish you'll need 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • Butter: This recipe calls for 1 cup of butter.
  • cream:The rich sauce calls for 3/4 pint heavy cream.
  • Seasonings: Seasonings include salt and pepper to taste and 1 dash of garlic salt .
  • Cheese: You'll need 3/4 cup romano and 1/2 parmesan cheeses.

Steps to prepare

  1. Step one: bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add Fettuccinni Pasta cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Step two:Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  3. Step three: Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
