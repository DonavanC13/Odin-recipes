The Irish Shepherd's pie

In this recipe we're ging to be making a true classic the Irish Shepherd's pie.

Shepherds pie is a dish consisting of mashed potatoes, carrots, peas, onions, minced meat and gravy this traditional irish dish usually calls for lamb, that said this particular recipe has been modified so that you can use either lamb or beef.

Ingredients

Meat filling:

  • 2 table spoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1lb. 90% lean ground beef - or ground lamb
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves -minced
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup frozen mixed peas & carrots*
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels
